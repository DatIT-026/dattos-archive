Introducing UrGift App

The new way to express your feelings to the one you love.

Confessing love with technology is a new and modern way compared to traditional methods. UrGift is a fun app that helps you express your feelings in a unique and exciting way. Based on the original confession website for Desktop, UrGift Android has a simple and optimized design. Some features from the Desktop version have been removed, but new and interesting ones have been added to make your confession even better.

Info

This is the mobile version of the Thu Khoa Ly Project, now renamed UrGift. This version is not only optimized for mobile user, but also includes new features not available in the web version.

Android 6.0 or higher required
